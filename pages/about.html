June 2024 – The Rise of a Vision.

  • Genkit was born out of a shared vision among eight close school friends, united by creativity, ambition, and a deep interest in technology. What started as casual brainstorming sessions soon evolved into something greater — a collaborative journey to build a startup that delivers impactful services.
  • Our first milestone? Building our own digital home — the Genkit website — a symbol of our unity, ambition, and the journey ahead.
  • With no set roadmap but boundless passion, we began our journey in June 2024 by envisioning and planning our digital home — a bold first step toward the future we dared to create.

Our Current Chapter

  • From sparks of imagination to real collaboration, Genkit has transformed from an idea into a growing force.
  • our eight-member team — bonded by years of friendship and a shared dream — is actively building our brand, shaping our digital presence, and laying the groundwork for innovative services.
  • This chapter marks more than progress; it’s a testament to our belief in learning, growing, and building — together. Every line of code, every idea shared, is a step closer to the future we envisioned.
